Our Story

For nearly two decades, Pro Nails 3 has been a dedicated family-owned local business that has provided the most quality and best services for all of our clients since 2002. We continue to strive to maintain the best facilities including: nail care, body care, and cleanliness. Our employees are made up of hard working immigrant families who have taken all measures to provide you with the best comfort and care possible.

*  *  *

During this pandemic, we have taken the most precautions requiring all employees and guests to wear a face mask and wash their hands. The facility is cleaned multiple times a day and handled with care. We are committed to maintaining cleanliness while still supplying services to our customers during COVID-19.

*  *  *

The products we use are carefully selected with the utmost intention of providing the best quality and premium products to our customers. Each product is cruelty free and personally tested. With that being said, we would love if you could join our family as we are always making our best efforts to provide customer satisfaction and a way to make our customers feel their best. Please come book with us at PRO NAILS 3!
